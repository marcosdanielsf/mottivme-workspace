# Browserbase Pricing Update (Accurate as of Nov 2025)

- **Cost**: $0/month
- **Concurrent browsers**: 1
- **Browser hours**: 1 hour included
- **Proxies**: 0 GB
- **Data retention**: 7 days
- **Session duration limit**: 15 minutes
- **Features**: Basic functionality
- **Support**: Email only

### Developer Plan
- **Cost**: $20/month
- **Concurrent browsers**: 25
- **Browser hours**: 100 hours included, then $0.12/hour
- **Proxies**: 1 GB included, then $12/GB
- **Data retention**: 30 days  
- **Session duration limit**: 6 hours
- **Features**: Basic Stealth Mode, Auto CAPTCHA solving
- **Support**: Standard support
- **Max projects**: 2

### Startup Plan (Most Popular)
- **Cost**: $99/month
- **Concurrent browsers**: 100
- **Browser hours**: 500 hours included, then $0.10/hour
- **Proxies**: 5 GB included, then $10/GB
- **Data retention**: 30 days
- **Session duration limit**: 6 hours
- **Features**: Basic Stealth Mode, Auto CAPTCHA solving
- **Support**: Priority support
- **Max projects**: 5

### Scale Plan
- **Cost**: Custom pricing
- **Concurrent browsers**: 250+

- **Proxies**: Usage-based

## Recommended Tiers for GHL Agent AI

### For Starter Tier (1-5 clients)
**Recommended**: Developer Plan ($20/month)
- **Why**: 25 concurrent browsers (5 per client), 100 hours/month (20 hours per client)
- **Use case**: Testing, light automation, proof of concept
- **Estimated usage**: 50-80 hours/month
- **Overage cost**: Minimal ($0.12/hour if exceeded)

### For Growth Tier (5-20 clients)  
**Recommended**: Startup Plan ($99/month)
- **Why**: 100 concurrent browsers (5-20 per client), 500 hours/month (25-100 hours per client)
- **Use case**: Production automation, multiple daily workflows
- **Estimated usage**: 300-450 hours/month
- **Overage cost**: $0.10/hour beyond 500 hours

### For Enterprise Tier (20+ clients)
**Recommended**: Scale Plan (Custom, est. $500-1,500/month)
- **Why**: 250+ concurrent browsers, unlimited usage, advanced features
- **Use case**: 24/7 automation, high-volume operations
- **Estimated usage**: 1,000+ hours/month
- **Features**: HIPAA compliance, SSO, dedicated support

## Final Recommendation

**Start with Developer Plan ($20/month)** for initial testing and proof of concept with 1-5 clients. This provides:
- 25 concurrent browsers (more than enough for testing)
- 100 hours/month (sufficient for light automation)
- All core features (stealth mode, CAPTCHA solving)
- Low risk investment

**Upgrade to Startup Plan ($99/month)** when:
- Managing 5+ active clients
- Running 200+ hours of automation monthly
- Need for 30+ concurrent sessions
- Require priority support

**Consider Scale Plan (Custom)** when:
- Managing 20+ clients with heavy automation
- Need HIPAA compliance or SSO
- Require 500+ hours monthly
- Want dedicated support and SLA
