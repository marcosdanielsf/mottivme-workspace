## Pricing Recommendations by Business Model

### 1. Self-Service SaaS (50-100% Margin)
- **Starter:** $182-244/client/month
- **Growth:** $95-126/client/month
- **Enterprise:** $52-69/client/month
- **Target:** High volume, low touch, automated onboarding

### 2. Managed Service (100-200% Margin)
- **Starter:** $244-365/client/month
- **Growth:** $126-189/client/month
- **Enterprise:** $69-104/client/month
- **Target:** Moderate volume, some support, training included

### 3. White-Label Partner (200-300% Margin)
- **Starter:** $365-487/client/month
- **Growth:** $189-252/client/month
- **Enterprise:** $104-138/client/month
- **Target:** Low volume, high touch, custom branding

### 4. Done-For-You Concierge (300%+ Margin)
- **Starter:** $487+/client/month
- **Growth:** $252+/client/month
- **Enterprise:** $138+/client/month
- **Target:** Premium clients, full-service automation

## Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)
- **Pricing:** 100% margin (balanced)
- **Target:** 10-20 early adopters
- **Goal:** $5K-10K MRR
- **Focus:** Product-market fit, testimonials

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-12)
- **Pricing:** 150-200% margin (premium)
- **Target:** 50-100 clients
- **Goal:** $25K-50K MRR
- **Focus:** Scaling infrastructure, case studies

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)
- **Pricing:** Tiered (50-300% based on service level)
- **Target:** 200-500 clients
- **Goal:** $100K+ MRR
- **Focus:** Enterprise contracts, white-label partnerships

## Key Takeaways

1. **Recommended Starting Price:** $243.60/client/month (Starter, 100% margin)
2. **Optimal Target:** 10-35 clients in first 6 months
3. **First-Year MRR Goal:** $25,000-50,000
4. **Scaling Path:** Start at 100% margin, increase to 200% as value is proven
5. **Enterprise Opportunity:** 150+ clients at $104/month = $15.5K MRR at 200% margin
