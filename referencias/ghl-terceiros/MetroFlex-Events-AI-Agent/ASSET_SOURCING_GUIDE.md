### Video Specifications for Hero Section

**Optimal Settings:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- Frame Rate: 30fps (24fps for vintage feel)
- Codec: H.264
- File Size: Under 10MB (compress if needed)
- Duration: 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- Format: MP4

**Post-Production Effects (Apply Before Upload):**
- Convert to black & white or sepia tone
- Add film grain overlay
- Reduce saturation by 70%
- Increase contrast by 10-20%
- Add subtle vignette
- Use free tools like:
  - DaVinci Resolve (Free)
  - Kapwing (Online, Free tier)
  - Canva Video Editor (Free)

## Creating Vintage/Film Grain Effects

### Free Tools for Video Effects

**1. DaVinci Resolve** (Free, Professional)
- Download: https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve
- Features: Color grading, film grain, vintage effects
- Tutorial: YouTube "DaVinci Resolve vintage film effect"

**2. Kapwing** (Online, Free Tier)
- URL: https://www.kapwing.com/
- Features: Filters, overlays, text
- Workflow: Upload → Apply "Vintage" filter → Export

**3. Canva Video Editor** (Free Tier)
- URL: https://www.canva.com/
- Features: Filters, text overlays
- Workflow: Upload video → Apply "Black & White" filter → Add grain texture → Export

### Film Grain Overlays (Free)

**Download Free Film Grain Textures:**
- Videezy: https://www.videezy.com/ (search "film grain overlay")
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/film%20grain/
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/film%20grain/

**How to Apply:**
1. Download film grain video overlay
2. Layer over your bodybuilding footage in video editor
3. Set blend mode to "Overlay" or "Screen"
4. Reduce opacity to 30-50%
5. Export as MP4
