## üì± Parallax Effects (Recommendation)

### Subtle Parallax (Recommended)

**Sections with parallax:**
1. **Hero background** - Moves slower than content (0.5x speed)
2. **Event cards** - Slight depth effect on scroll
3. **Stats numbers** - Count up when scrolled into view
4. **Legacy section background** - Slow parallax gradient

**Implementation:**
- Lightweight JavaScript (no libraries needed)
- Smooth 60fps animations
- Mobile-optimized (reduced effects on small screens)
- Reduced motion support (respects accessibility)

**Effect:**
- Adds depth and premium feel
- Not distracting or heavy
- Enhances storytelling
